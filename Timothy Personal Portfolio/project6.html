This was the last game I worked on during my second year of university, which took me seven weeks to complete. Our client was a class of 5th graders, and we had to turn their drawing of monsters into an actual game. We had three artists, two engineers and two designers working on the design together. The end result is a fun puzzle game where you guide your monster buddies through different landscapes and eventually beat the boss. The work not only showcases our technical and creative capabilities, it also shows how we work together in a team and on a tight deadline.
