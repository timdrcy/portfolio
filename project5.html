Together, with the help of two designers, three artists, and one engineer, we wrote the story of Lily. She’s a young girl who’s been neglected and abused, finding solace in nightmares about animals that embody her traumas and fears. Through a voice like her grandfather’s, she fights off these beasts, finding her own strength. This game combines storytelling with Metroidvania-style gameplay to take players on an emotional journey of discovery and triumph.
